# 🏔️ Pashto Info Repository

Welcome to the **Pashto Info** repository! 🚀
This project is dedicated to sharing information about Pashto culture, traditions, clothing, food, and civilization.

---

## 📖 About This Project

This repository highlights the rich and beautiful culture of the Pashtun people. It includes information about:

* 👕 Pashto Traditional Clothing
* 🍲 Pashto Foods & Cuisine
* 🏛️ Pashto Customs & Traditions
* 🌄 Pashto Civilization & History
* 🎉 Cultural Events & Practices
* 📚 And much more cultural knowledge

---

## 🌟 Purpose

* Preserve and share Pashto culture
* Educate people about Pashtun traditions
* Promote cultural awareness
* Provide easy-to-read cultural information

---

## 🧵 Topics Covered

### 👕 Clothing

Traditional Pashto dresses, styles, and cultural wear.

### 🍛 Food

Famous Pashto dishes, recipes, and culinary traditions.

### 🏛️ Customs

Marriage traditions, hospitality, and social practices.

### 🌄 Civilization

History, lifestyle, and cultural evolution of Pashtuns.
